Factorization

  • Symmetric Nonnegative Matrix Factorization for Graph Clustering (SDM 2012)
    • Da Kuang, Chris Ding, and Haesun Park [Paper]
    • 了解$\text{Non-negative matrix factorization(NMF)}$的性质，优化目标是$$\min\limits_{C,G\geqslant 0}\parallel X-CG^T\parallel^2$$$X\in \mathbb{R}^{m\times n},C\in \mathbb{R}^{m\times k},G\in \mathbb{R}^{n\times k}$；$X$表示$n$个$m$维的数据，$k$是类别数，分解后得到的$C$表示$k$个簇中心向量，$G$表示$n$个样本所属于的类别：$G_{kj}>G_{ij},\forall i\ne k$，则$v_j\in \mathcal{C}_k$。
    • 对于$\text{NMF}$来说，当数据在空间内是非线性时，$\text{NMF}$会失效，即无法很好的确定$k$个中心向量。在这种情况下，我们可以用相似性矩阵去表示$X$。即优化$$\min\limits_{H\geqslant 0}\parallel A-HH^T\parallel^2$$
    • 该方法需要先确定好$k$
  • Community Detection via Measure Space Embedding (NIPS 2015)
    • Mark Kozdoba and Shie Mannor [Paper] [Python Reference]
    • $A=\{a_{ij}\}\text{ is the symmetric adjacency matrix of G}$
    • $T=D^{-1}A\text{ is the transition matrix of the random walk on G}$
    • $\pi(i)=\frac{d_i}{\sum_j d_j} \text{ is the stationary measure of the random walk}$，用于初始化。
    • $w_i=\frac{1}{L}\left(w_i^1+…+w_i^L\right)$
    • $\mu_S=\frac{1}{\sum_{i\in S}d_i}\sum_{i\in S}d_iw_i$
    • $D(\nu,\mu)=\sum_{i\in V}\nu(i)\log\mu(i)$
    • $\text{Algorithm Diffusion Entropy Reducer:}$
      1. $\text{Input: Graph G,walk length L,number of component k}$
      2. $\text{Compute the measures }w_i$
      3. $\text{Initialize }P_1…P_k$
      4. $\text{repeat:}$
        1. $\mu_s=\mu_{P_s}\forall s\leq k$
        2. $\forall s\leq k,\text{set}$$$P_s=\left\{i\in V\mid s=\arg\max\limits_{l}D(w_i,\mu_l)\right\}$$
      5. $\text{until the sets }P_s\text{ do not change}$
    • 没完全看懂，但该方法依然需要先确定好$k$
    • 评价指标：$\text{the normalized mutual information(NMI)}:$$$NMI(P,Q)=2\frac{I(P,Q)}{H(P)+H(Q)}$$

但对于我们的挖掘项目往往无法确定$k$值，如何不依赖于$k$？下面两篇文章主要讲如何选择或者预估$k$值。

  • Cross-Validation Estimate of the Number of Clusters in a Network (Scientific Report 2017)
    • Matsuro Kawamoto and Yoshiyuki Kabashima [Paper]
  • Comparative Analysis on the Selection of Number of Clusters in Community Detection (ArXiv 2017)
    • Matsuro Kawamoto and Yoshiyuki Kabashima [Paper]

同时在我们的项目中有一些正样本，即存在一些关系对，该关系对的两个元素属于同一个簇，如何利用这些信息进行半监督式的挖掘。
